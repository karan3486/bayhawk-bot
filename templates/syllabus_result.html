Syllabus for "Blockchain Technology for Beginners"

# Course Overview

- **Name:** Blockchain Technology for Beginners - **Weekly Sessions:** 2 sessions - **Hours per Session:** 2 hours - **Semester Duration:** 4 months

# Week 1

- **Topic:** Introduction to Blockchain Technology - **Activities:** - What is Blockchain? - History of Blockchain - Applications of Blockchain - Class Discussion

# Week 2

- **Topic:** Basics of Cryptography in Blockchain - **Activities:** - Cryptographic Hash Functions - Public and Private Keys - Digital Signatures - Practical Exercises

# Week 3

- **Topic:** Understanding Blockchain Networks - **Activities:** - P2P Networks - Consensus Mechanisms - Types of Blockchains - Q&A Session

# Week 4

- **Topic:** Smart Contracts and Decentralized Applications (DApps) - **Activities:** - Introduction to Smart Contracts - Ethereum and Solidity Basics - Decentralized Application Development - Hands-on Coding Session

# Week 5

- **Topic:** Blockchain Security and Privacy - **Activities:** - Security Risks in Blockchain - Privacy Challenges - Best Practices - Case Studies

# Week 6

- **Topic:** Blockchain Use Cases - **Activities:** - Supply Chain Management - Voting Systems - Healthcare Applications - Guest Speaker Session

# Midterm Review

- **Assessment:** Midterm Evaluation and Review of Concepts (Month 2)

# Week 7

- **Topic:** Tokenization and Digital Assets - **Activities:** - Token Standards - Initial Coin Offerings (ICOs) - Security Tokens vs. Utility Tokens - Regulatory Landscape

# Week 8

- **Topic:** Blockchain Scalability and Interoperability - **Activities:** - Scalability Challenges - Sharding and Layer 2 Solutions - Interoperability Protocols - Group Project Initiation

# Week 9

- **Topic:** Future Trends in Blockchain Technology - **Activities:** - DeFi (Decentralized Finance) - NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) - Blockchain and IoT - Research Presentations

# Week 10

- **Topic:** Legal and Ethical Implications of Blockchain - **Activities:** - Regulatory Environment - Data Privacy Laws - Ethics in Blockchain - Debate Session

# Week 11

- **Topic:** Blockchain Implementation Strategies - **Activities:** - Enterprise Adoption - Case Studies - Proof-of-Concept Development - Progress Report Presentation

# Final Week

- **Topic:** Final Project Presentations and Course Reflection - **Activities:** - Group Project Presentations - Course Evaluation - Q&A and Wrap-Up This syllabus ensures comprehensive coverage of blockchain fundamentals for beginners within a 4-month timeframe, allowing for a structured and engaging learning experience.
